## {{page.title}}

I have said that any changes, earth changes that occur will be evidences of
healing. And will therefore be greater and greater evidences of harmony. So do
not buy into the crepe hanging that is going on. I encourage you. To look at
this world. With the curiosity to see it for what it really is, which is the
Kindom of Heaven.

Love it by not projecting upon it your fears. It’s just like your body. You
don’t want to project on it distrust, that it on its own can act at odds with
you and not in your best interests. That’s not love, that’s hate. Your body is
the visibility and tangibility of your individuality, and your individuality
is the presence of God. I didn’t say your ego was your individuality is the
presence of God.

Therefore, great appreciation and honoring and respect needs to be expressed
by you or embraced by you relative to your body, and the same thing with your
world, because the only way you’re going to see your body as it divinely is
and the only way you’re going to see the Kindom of Heaven, that you’re
currently calling the world as the Kindom of Heaven, is when you are looking
to see the Kindom of Heaven there instead of some prediction coming true. You
see?
