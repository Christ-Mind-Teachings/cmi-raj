is far from a flight of fancy that is significant, that you will be
changing and growing. And that it’s appropriate for it to be graceful rather
than frightening or difficult. So now you’ve been given a little bit of a hint
that later on a more direct approach to God is in the cards for you. It

has value in reducing your fear of your good. But nevertheless, it’s the
Father’s good pleasure to give you the Kingdom, and he’s gifting you with it

And so I want you to realize that a connection with God can happen at any
moment that you’re undefended against it. And it doesn’t have to be way down
the road. God is love. And you don’t need to be afraid of it. God is appearing
to you right now. Remember that when you look at your brother, whether your
brother is consciously allowing the Christ expression which is the Christ
