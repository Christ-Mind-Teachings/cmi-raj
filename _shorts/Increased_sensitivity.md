## {{page.title}}

Oh, and that increased sensitivity. It makes it so easy to get pissed off at
everyone else’s ignorance which makes it very easy for you to become defended
again and turn your back on the altar. As you see more clearly things that
hadn’t bothered you before didn’t register with you before.

And as you become clearer, it’s easier to see how unclear others still are. So
you must be very careful that because of your increased capacity to be the
presence of love because you opened up to it does not become sidetracked by
judgments resulting from seeing that your friends, your kindred spirits are
more ignorant than you thought they were.

Greater clarity and the willingness to be the fuller presence of love will
uncover to you just how much love is needed. And the temptation will be to
turn your back on the altar. And be bothered, disgusted, depressed by how
great the ignorance is.
