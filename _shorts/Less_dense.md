## {{page.title}}

The density of the dream has lessened with every little insight that any one
experiences. And any instantaneous healing that occurs causes such a shift in
the experience and the mind of the one who had the healing that it touches
everyone else as well. And it becomes easier for that to happen for everyone
else. But it can’t make anyone accept the truth.
