## {{page.title}}

To recognize what is true and nothing else constitutes the very nature of
Being with a capital B, your true Being. And just as a habit isn’t dropped
lightly and requires of you, persistence, as a new habit develops you will find
yourself consciously practicing judgment. Meaning the recognition of what is
of value and nothing else. You will do that with persistence. But not because
you’re any longer overcoming an inclination to go back to the old habit. It
will seem to go on for a great deal of time until time leaves your awareness.
Until time is abolished. So don’t be discouraged. Awakening can happen now.
