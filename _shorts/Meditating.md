## {{page.title}}

From that place that you arrive at as a result of meditating you are primed for being able to easily hear the answer when you ask God, “what is it appropriate for me to do? What is your viewpoint of this?”

In the quietness that one comes to as a result of meditating the defenses that get in the way or create the static that makes it difficult to hear your guidance it’s so reduced. It’s so much less when you have meditated that hearing is easier and establishing the connection is easier.

Each one of you is worth 20 minutes twice a day to disregard everything else and sit down and become still. Every one of you is worth 40 minutes out of your day where you’re not taking care of somebody else. OK.
