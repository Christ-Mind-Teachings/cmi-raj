## {{page.title}}

No. The message is that there’s somewhere other to be then inside the prison,
you see. But if you think that what’s going to come is going to be like what
already came and that it’s just going to be a new message for you to write on
the wall, there will be no release. No liberation, you see. So you’ve got to
listen with totally fresh ears. Because the answers will come in totally fresh
language with meanings that haven’t dawned on you yet.
