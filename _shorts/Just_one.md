## {{page.title}}

One with God is a majority. It really only takes one to be clear. An illusion
equals 0. So you can put a million zeros together and they all add up to 0,
even though they seem to have what you might call mass or size or take up a
certain amount of space. Overcoming nothing is no problem. And it only takes
one to see that it’s nothing.

For one reason or another, something happens in them that allows them to
overlook the costume meaning the apparent circumstances. And see through it to
the truth. And the behavior of a child. It’s not impossible. Don’t don’t use
your mind to create an overwhelming image that says, “well, it must take more
than one person finding his or her center. And being open to the influx of
God’s perspective and his love.“

It doesn’t. It just takes one.
