## {{page.title}}

To be an effective teacher is to not attempt to be a teacher at all. But to be
uh, a humble servant. Humility is a state in which there’s no tension.
Humility is a state in which the capacity to be forceful doesn’t exist.
Humility is where willfulness has been laid aside for willingness. Willingness
to say “thy will be done.”

Why? Not so you can be controlled. But so that in the absence of your trying
to be controlling the divine way things work can register with you, and you
can be in harmony with it. And thus be a presence who is constantly glorifying
God by not glorifying anything else. In the absence of the attempt to glorify
anything else God is the only thing that can show up.
