## {{page.title}}

A lot of involvement which everyone is worthy of, and for whom you have a gift
to give. Do it willingly. Because it is the means you have of extending love.
And extending love in the most practical and down to earth ways that meet
real needs, sharing the love that you are, that you have to give. Yes, and
thanking them for the opportunity to have an object of your affection.
