## {{page.title}}

You’ve got to become involved. And you’ve got to say, “Father, show me how to
love him.” Because the love has to be a gift in a relationship. It has to be
the manifestation of caring. It has to be the manifestation of joining in
brotherhood for reasons of brotherhood.

This, this, this globe, this planet is not going to arrive at peace and
harmony until the reasons everyone relates to each other are reasons of love
and brotherhood to express and embody brotherhood.
