## {{page.title}}

It’s all always just one thing. It’s always the presence of God. And the
presence of God there called you, can experience it’s Godness or it can
pretend that it has a separate viewpoint of its own, and be confused. But
never for a moment has anything but the presence of God been there.

So the process of awakening is a process of the one and only Christ there is.
The one and only direct expression of God there is called you becoming clear
about the fact that you are the one and only direct expression of God there is
appearing as you. And becoming clear about the fact that the physical material
world and universe is the Kingdom of Heaven misperceived. It’s about one
thing, moving from confusion into clarity, coming home into your right mind.
Into your sanity.
