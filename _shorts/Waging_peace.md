## {{page.title}}

Now that’s your job. To bring peace to other minds. It’s your job to wage
peace. Instead of war. Waging peace. Bringing peace to the minds of others is
accomplished by saying no. To everything that is unlike peace.

It is a gentle spirit. Capable of unequivocally saying no to that which
doesn’t represent peace. To that which doesn’t embody peace. And then in peace
providing an irresistible invitation. To your brother to join you in that
peace. And in the attitude of love that ensures that peace will be embodied
further.

I could put it this way, teachers of peace are not wimps. Teachers of peace
are able to stand in the face of calls for love that appear to be attack.
Without being swayed by the presentation. But with the capability to
unswervingly say no. An absolute no to that which doesn’t support peace. So
that the educational expression of love and intelligence can occur and
transform.
