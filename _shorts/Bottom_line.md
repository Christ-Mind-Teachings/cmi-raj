## {{page.title}}

At the bottom line, everybody wants to be loved. Everyone wants to be
respected. Everyone wants to be able to feel love for everyone else. Everyone
wants to feel safe in giving, and receiving love, and in being respected.
That’s it at the bottom line. And if you’re all the direct expression of God
and therefore, the Christ plain, pure and simple, then you are all worthy of
respect, and love, and kindness, and nothing less.
